About Us

Welcome to MentIIT! We are a team of four final-year students from IIT Gandhinagar who have developed this platform with a mission to support and guide students who have obtained lower ranks and are unsure about their chances of securing a college.

We understand the complexities of the Jossa counselling process, and that's why we have built a strong community of 43 IITians and 38 NITians from all across India. Our platform enables students to connect with knowledgeable individuals from specific colleges, ensuring that any doubts or queries regarding a particular college can be addressed directly by students studying there. We strive to empower students with the right information and assistance, so they can make informed decisions and achieve their academic goals.

At MentIIT, our focus is on reaching local coaching institutes and schools. Help us connect with them, so we can provide valuable support to students in making informed college decisions. Together, let's empower the next generation of achievers.
